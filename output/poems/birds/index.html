BIRDS

One moment I’m a philosopher, all words and fat with truth. The next I’m a poet moonlighting for something new to say. I’ve tried on the certainty of equations since they promised me the last word. But I liked the birds instead, every word of theirs a spell that guarantees the truth of my existence, that truths are truisms only sometimes true, that the truth lies within and beyond what can be said with words.
