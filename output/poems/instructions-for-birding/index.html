INSTRUCTIONS FOR BIRDING

It’s not all that different from living well. Go slow. Leave your labels at home. Sit in the unknown and let the silence speak.

Wait. Wait a little longer. Wait until you remember: a thing can travel everywhere just by holding still.

Clear that from your mind. Forget the existence of time. You have all day which is enough for a lifetime.

Listen. Look. Look again until you can see the shape of sound.
