MORNING

In the nook of this morning there is the urge to be busy. There is meaning wanting to be found again. But the golden retriever has hopped between two humans musing about the sun. How difficult it is, she thinks, to be so thoughtful. Her neck cranes downward toward the dirty floorboards, takes a whiff of what is missing and suddenly remembers the shape of dirt.
