---
title: "Dad"
date: 2021-07-29
tags: [poems]
title-block-banner: false
---

Hunched over in your garden
like a daylily on the boardwalk
watching the world spin faster
and faster on its slant axis,
you still water the plants
we bought for you
three summers ago
as if one day
we will grow
and turn back
in your direction.
