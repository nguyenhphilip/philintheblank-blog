---
title: "Morning"
date: 2022-04-29
tags: [poems]
title-block-banner: false
---

In the nook of this morning
there is the urge to be
busy. There is meaning
wanting to be found again.
But the golden retriever
has hopped between two humans
musing about the sun. How difficult
it is, she thinks, to be so
thoughtful. Her neck cranes
downward toward the dirty
floorboards, takes a whiff
of what is missing
and suddenly remembers
the shape of dirt.
