---
title: "Trapeze"
date: 2020-02-02
tags: [poems]
title-block-banner: false
---

The only way this works
is if we catch each other.

We’re experienced, yes
but the connection is never

guaranteed even after all
these years. The nets below 

don’t carry the weight of
our love, nor does the applause

of the crowd. They wonder 
how we do what we do, why

we take the risks we take.
But we’re just doing our job

putting on a performance
for an audience of one.

Sometimes we fall but
that’s a part of the deal –

we’re acrobats of the dark
she said, two loose cannons

shooting for what's above
us; we're trapeze artists

vaulting into the dark,
arms open wide.
