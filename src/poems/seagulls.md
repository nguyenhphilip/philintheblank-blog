---
title: "Seagulls"
date: 2019-05-05
tags: [poems]
title-block-banner: false
---

Two birds squaked along a grayed shore
like inseparable twins talking

and squealing in that terrifying tongue
only they can understand, mocking

our efforts to tame the unknown, laughing
because we can’t when the waves come.

But for all our differences
I was reassured to find

that they shit all the same
as we do, with our eyes toward

the sky, thinking of anything but
what is happening beneath us.
