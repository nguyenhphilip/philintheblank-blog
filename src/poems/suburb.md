title: "Suburban Dinner"
date: 2025-11-24
tags: [poems]
draft: false
---

Not much was said aside the usual
chuff on the price of beef and lamb,
how it never gets any cheaper. The father
shrugs it off with a resigned laugh, skewing
the bowl with his chopsticks while the mother
spoons rice into her mouth. When the dishes
are clean again the father moves himself
into the garage where he watches videos
about the history of his people as the mother flicks
through the sale section of an online shop.
The sons go upstairs to play video games
in separate rooms, to fight
unknown enemies keeping themselves alive
against others doing the same.
