---
title: "Waiting at the airport"
date: 2025-11-20
tags: [poems]
draft: false
---

How to tell you this
is how the good days go: I sit
and wait, wait and listen
until something stirs the soup
of my insides, disturbs and excites
all at once like the carbine engine
of a plane coming to life to take us
elsewhere. Behind me a silver-haired
baby buzzes her bright lips, is becoming
something that can fly without wings.
