---
title: "My Light"
date: 2020-06-30
tags: [poems]
title-block-banner: false
---

When someone dies
are they really gone?

The apples in the garden
miss your touch.

And the Earth it seems
can't weep enough.

Forever feels more
real than the rain

does. You're gone
but the story

lives on
in a bed

of neurons, impulses,
images

with enough power
to light universes.

I wish you were here
but you are there

where stars like you
are meant to burn

like diamonds studded
in the sky,

your bright
presence forever

my light, life
of the night.
