What rooms of the soul
have we left unchecked?
Forgotten and locked away
With the key nowhere
to be found? Look: the door
is still open. Climb
the creaking stairs,
open the drawers
and peek inside; run
your fingers along the shelves
now draped in dust somewhere
in the attic of your mind.
You might rediscover the very thing
you have been searching for
your entire life. The ghosts
will cry and beckon.
Don't run. Sit and stay
awhile. Get to know yourself.
Offer company and light
to the strange
and stranger.
