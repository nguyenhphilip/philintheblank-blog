((What if)&emsp;(the sun)&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(was an eyeball)
&nbsp;(cut)&emsp;(in)(pieces)&emsp;(Would each one)&emsp;&emsp;(blink)
&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(or flicker&emsp;)(&emsp;like a flame)
&emsp;(like)(a firefly)&nbsp;(:)&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(Tiny summer flares) 

&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(Accretions)(of dust)&emsp;&emsp;(Feeling arpeggiated
&emsp;across)&emsp;&emsp;(guitar)(strings)&emsp;&emsp;(Thoughts)
&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&nbsp;(of you)&emsp;&emsp;(bifurcating)&emsp;&emsp;(Perfume)
&emsp;(in motion)&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(Yes)&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(it’s better&emsp;&emsp;than cinema)

&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(Just try)&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(to catch it&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;all
I)(’m)(jarred)&emsp;(just thinking)(about it)&emsp;&emsp;(It is
)&emsp;(the closest)(we’ll get)&emsp;(to seeing)&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(the birth
&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;and death&emsp;&emsp;of stars)

&emsp;&emsp;(What happens)&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(next)&emsp;(Is)(this)
&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(the (end)ing))&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;&emsp;(?)
