---
title: "I long for you"
date: 2025-12-03
tags: [poems]
draft: false
---

I long for you.
I long to come home
and rest on your shoulder
wordlessly, no questions asked,
I have been questioning
myself, the world
my whole life.
Take me in, hold me
gently like a feather who has
at last found the Earth,
hush my dried lips
with your finger
which says: no more.
Not now. Right now it is time 
for no time. We will go
and bathe in skin
and silence.
We will dwell
in the dark
land of children
who know nothing
of work. Who know
only play and the labor
of love pulsing through
their grass-stained hands,
hands they press daily
into each other whether
fighting or reaching
for the places they have
yet to discover words for.
