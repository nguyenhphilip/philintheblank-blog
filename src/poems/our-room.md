Pens and paintbrushes
scattered on the bathroom floor.
Half-finished poems and crooked photos
line the chipping walls. In the dark
corners our two desks rest.
The candles are beyond extinguished,
our eyesight not the best.
Loved ones tell us they're concerned.
They say we need something different.
But still we can see
we have all that we need, everything
we can dream we do and more
behind the inner door
they cannot find.
